ORDER 
THIS MATTER is before the undersigned following a review of the case. On 
September 7, 2022, the Court (Willocks, J.) granted a motion filed by Kyle R. Waldner, 
Esq. on August 25, 2022, asking permission to withdraw as attorney for Plaintiff 
Ghislaine Maxwell. The order granting Attorney Waldner's motion was entered the next 
day, on September 8, 2022, and a copy was served on Ms. Maxwell on September 15, 
2022. To date, Ms. Maxwell has not responded to the order and her case remains 
pending without movement. To determine if Ms. Maxwell intends to pursue her claims, 
the undersigned will hold a hearing. 
Accordingly, it is hereby 
ORDERED that this matter is scheduled for a remote Hearing before the 
undersigned on Friday, March 17, 2023 at 10:00 a.m. AST (9:00 a.m. ET) via Microsoft 
Teams. As Ms. Maxwell is, at present, without an attorney, the undersigned directs the
