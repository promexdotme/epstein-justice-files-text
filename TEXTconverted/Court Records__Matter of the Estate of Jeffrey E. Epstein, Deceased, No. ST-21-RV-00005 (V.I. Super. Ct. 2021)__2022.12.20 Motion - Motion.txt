MOTION TO WITHDRAW MOTION FOR AWARD OF ATTORNEYS’ FEES 
COME NOW the Co-Executors of the Estate of Jeffrey E. Epstein (the “Epstein 
Estate”), DARREN K. INDYKE and RICHARD D. KAHN (the “Co-Executors”), and hereby 
move this Honorable Court to withdraw their Motion for Award of Attorneys’ Fees filed on 
March 4, 2022 and docketed on March 18, 2022.  The ground for the instant motion is that the 
parties have reached a resolution in the related Govt of USVI v. Epstein Estate et al., ST-2020-
CV-00014 matter, and in conjunction with that the Co-Executors have agreed to withdraw their 
request for attorneys’ fees in this action.  A proposed order is attached for the Court’s 
consideration.
