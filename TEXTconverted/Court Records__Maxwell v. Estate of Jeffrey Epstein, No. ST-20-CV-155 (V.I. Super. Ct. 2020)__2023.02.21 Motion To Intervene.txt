GOVERNMENT OF THE UNITED STATES VIRGIN ISLANDS’  
WITHDRAWAL OF MOTION TO INTERVENE 
 
 
The Government of the United States Virgin Islands (“Government”), by and through its 
undersigned counsel, hereby withdraws it Motion to Intervene, which was filed in the above-
captioned action on July 10, 2020, in light of the Government’s recent settlement of its separately-
filed action against Defendant the Estate of Jeffrey E. Epstein.
