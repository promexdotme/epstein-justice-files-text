O R D E R 
THIS MATTER is before the Court on the Co-Executors of the Estate of Jeffrey E. 
Epstein, Darren K. Indyke and Richard D. Kahn’s Motion to Withdraw Motion for Award of 
Attorneys’ Fees.  Having considered the Motion, the Court will grant the same.
Accordingly, it is hereby
ORDERED that the Co-Executors of the Estate of Jeffrey E. Epstein, Darren K. Indyke 
and Richard D. Kahn’s Motion to Withdraw Motion for Award of Attorneys’ Fees is GRANTED; 
it is further
ORDERED that a copy of this Order shall be directed to counsel of record.
