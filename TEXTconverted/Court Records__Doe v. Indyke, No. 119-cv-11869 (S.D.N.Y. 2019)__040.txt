JOINT STIPULATION FOR DISMISSAL 
 
Plaintiff, ANASTASIA DOE, and Defendants, Darren K. Indyke and Richard D. Kahn, as 
Co-Executors of the Estate of Jeffrey E. Epstein, by and through their undersigned counsel, hereby 
stipulate and agree that due to Plaintiff’s resolution of her claim(s) against Defendants via the 
Epstein Victims’ Compensation Program, this action shall be DISMISSED WITH PREJUDICE, 
with each party to bear its own attorneys' fees and costs.
